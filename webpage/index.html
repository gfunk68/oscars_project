This year, the 92nd Academy Awards saw the first non-English-language film win Best Picture. In recent years many have criticized the Academy Awards for its lack of diversity in crowning women and people of color for some of its most coveted prizes, particularly Best Director and Best Actors and Actresses awards. A viral call-to-action hashtag, #OscarsSoWhite turned into a social movement to spread awareness about lack of diversity at the Oscars.

Thus, we were inspired to create some visualizations to learn more about previous Oscar winners.
